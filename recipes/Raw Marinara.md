Raw Marinara
* Ingredients:
- 1 C Sun-Dried Tomatoes (soaked in 1 C water until soft with the soaking water)
- 1 C Tomatoes (chopped)
- 3 T Olive Oil
- 2 T Fresh Oregano (minced)
- 2 T Braggs
- 1 tsp Fresh Rosemary
- 1/2 tsp Fresh Thyme
- 2 Cloves Garlic

* Method:
1. Blend until smooth.
