Nut Pate
* Ingredients:
- 2 C walnuts
- 2 stalks Celery
- 1 Red Bell Pepper
- 1 Scallion
- 1/2 tsp Sea Salt

* Method:
1. Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.
