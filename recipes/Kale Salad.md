Kale Salad
* Ingredients
- Kale
- Balsamic Vinegar
- Olive Oil
- Garlic (minced)
- Ginger (minced)
- Sesame Seeds
- Raisins

* Method:
1. Whisk the oil and vinegar to emulsify and let it sit with the garlic and ginger for a little while.
2. Toss all ingredients together.
